Project Site Plan

Site Name:

Wasatch Strength Training Club

Site Purpose:

This site provides a hub for the Strength Training community in the regional/county area providing close gyms, events, diet recommendations, information about training, lifestyle and benefits with an application form.

Scenarios:

  • - What are some of the places where I can train in the Wasatch County?

  • - Where can I find information about the club leaders?

  • - What are the benefits to changing my lifestyle?

  • - How can I make friends in this community?
